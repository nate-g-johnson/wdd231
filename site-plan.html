Fort Mill Hiking Trails Site Plan

Site Name

Fort Mill Hiking Trails — This name clearly identifies the local nature of the site and emphasizes hiking opportunities in the Fort Mill, South Carolina area. It’s simple, memorable, and geographically descriptive. Optional domain availability: fortmilltrails.org

Site Purpose

The site will provide an informative hub for hiking enthusiasts in and around Fort Mill, SC. It will include trail maps, descriptions, safety tips, parking and access details, and seasonal recommendations. It will also feature a contact form for users to submit trail updates or report issues.

Scenarios

  • What are the best family-friendly trails near Fort Mill with restroom facilities?
  • Where can I find detailed maps and current trail conditions for Fort Mill hiking areas?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color (Forest Green - #2e8b57): Used for main headings and navigation bar.
  • Accent Color (Desert Orange - #f4a261): Used for buttons, links, and section highlights.

Typography

  • Heading Font: Montserrat — Used for page and section headings.
  • Body Font: Open Sans — Used for paragraphs and general content.
